About JP Plumbing & Heating

Twelve years of honest, reliable service to the people of Cardiff.

Our Story

Built on Trust, Run by Locals

JP Plumbing & Heating started in 2012 with a single van, one engineer and a simple belief: that local people deserve honest, dependable tradespeople who turn up when they say they will.

More than a decade later, we're proud to be one of Cardiff's most trusted plumbing and heating firms. We've grown into a close-knit team of Gas Safe registered engineers, but our values haven't changed one bit — fair pricing, quality workmanship, and treating every home as if it were our own.

From emergency repairs at midnight to complete bathroom transformations, we bring the same care and professionalism to every job, large or small.
